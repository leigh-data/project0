About 13 from 6

13 from 6 is a gallery of 13 images from 6 different cities: Amsterdam, Munchen, Paris, London, Philadelphia, and Wildwood. All images were specially curated for this one of a kind portfolio.


All original images in the gallery were photographed by Leigh Michael Forrest. All logos in Things belong to and are linked to their respective trademark owners.
